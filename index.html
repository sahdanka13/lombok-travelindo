TOP 1

Gili Trawangan

Gili Trawangan is the third largest island to the northwest of Lombok. This island is famous for its enchanting underwater natural beauty. Even though it is not as busy as Carita beach in Anyer, Banten, Gili Trawangan still offers tranquility that is hard to find in other tourist attractions. On Gili Trawangan, tourists can do various exciting activities such as snorkeling, diving, sunbathing, swimming, sitting relaxing on the beach, and hunting for cool photos to add to their album gallery on social media. If you want to stay overnight, there are many accommodations such as villas, hotels and resorts around the beach area.

TOP 2

Tangsi Pink Beach

Pink Beach is one of the most famous beaches in Lombok. This beach is famous because it has unique pink sand. The uniqueness of this beach makes it one of the most recommended tourist attractions on Lombok Island. This beach is located in Sekaroh Village, Jerowaru District, East Lombok Regency, West Nusa Tenggara. Even though its location is hidden and unspoiled, this makes the most beautiful spot in the Sekaroh area, which is often called Tangsi beach, one of the most recommended tourist attractions in NTB. When visiting this virgin beach, don't forget to bring a camera and make sure to immortalize your beautiful moments through photos or videos with a pink beach as the background.

TOP 3

Tanjung Aan Beach

Tanjung Aan Beach is located in Pengembur Village, Sengkol District, Central Lombok Regency, West Nusa Tenggara. This beach is famous for having unique sand, like pepper grains. This sand is actually fossils from coral rocks. Walking on this expanse of sand like pepper grains is very healthy for the body because when you step on it, the nerves in the soles of your feet will be touched. Tanjung Aan Beach also has beautiful views, with white sand contrasting with the turquoise sea water.
